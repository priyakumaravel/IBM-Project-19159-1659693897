About Plasma Donation


      Plasma donation involves drawing blood, extracting the plasma, 
      and returning the rest of the blood to the donor through a process 
      called plasmapheresis. This type of donation takes slightly longer 
      than whole blood, but as a person is only donating plasma, it yields 
      a larger volume of blood plasma, meaning people can donate more frequently.
